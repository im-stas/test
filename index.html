FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can a trainer come to my home?

Most owners are matched within 2 hours and have a session booked within 24–48 hours. Same-day visits are often possible in metro service areas.

What methods do you use?

Positive, science-backed reinforcement. We're proudly LIMA (Least Intrusive, Minimally Aversive) and never use shock, prong, or pain-based tools.

Do you work with reactive or aggressive dogs?

Yes — our Behavior Rehab program is built for it. Every plan starts with a full assessment and, when appropriate, a partnership with your vet or veterinary behaviorist.

What's your service area?

In-home sessions are available across the greater metro region. Virtual coaching is available anywhere in the U.S. and Canada.

Is there a guarantee?

If your first session isn't the right fit, it's on us. We'll either re-match you with a different trainer or refund the visit — no friction.
