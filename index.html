What We Do

Complete care for every set of eyes

From routine exams to surgical co-management, we offer the full range of services to protect and improve your vision.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Computer-assisted exams that evaluate your vision and overall eye health with precision and comfort.

Contact Lens Fittings

Expert fittings for contact lenses of all types, so you find the option that fits your eyes and lifestyle.

Laser & Cataract Co-Management

Coordinated co-management for LASIK, refractive, and cataract surgery — before, during, and after your procedure.

Glaucoma Management

Ongoing monitoring and treatment plans to detect and manage glaucoma and other sight-threatening conditions.

Advanced Diagnostic Imaging

Digital photography, corneal topography, and optic nerve & retinal tomography for early, accurate detection.

Frame Styling

Personalized styling help to find frames that match your face, your prescription, and your personality.
